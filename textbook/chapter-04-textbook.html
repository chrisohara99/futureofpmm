Chapter Four

The Full Stack PMM

What separates the PMMs who compound from those who just keep up

"The Full Stack PMM is not a generalist with tools. They are a deep specialist who has extended their reach using AI—and redesigned their day around the difference."

This chapter defines the Full Stack PMM—the profile that compounds quarter over quarter while others plateau. It's not about learning more tools. It's about combining deep craft expertise with AI-enabled horizontal reach.

Most PMMs fall into one of two traps: deep expertise without AI leverage, or broad tool fluency without genuine craft. The Full Stack PMM escapes both—and the gap widens every quarter.

Key Insight

Time spent on narrative and positioning work doubles when PMMs redesign their workflow around AI. The commodity work drops from approximately 50% to approximately 10% of total effort.

The Problem

There are two ways to fail as a PMM in the AI era, and both are more common than they should be.

The Pure Specialist

Deep expertise, no AI leverage. Knows the domain cold but every deliverable still moves at human speed. AI makes this profile less competitive over time.

The AI Generalist

Broad tool fluency, shallow craft. Impressive personal stack, but actual domain expertise is thin. AI amplifies what you bring—if what you bring is generic, AI makes you generically faster.

The two traps are mirrors of each other. Pure Specialists have depth but not leverage. AI Generalists have leverage but not depth. The Full Stack PMM escapes both.

The Stack Architecture

The Full Stack PMM operates across three layers, each with distinct relationships to judgment:

Figure 4.4: The Three-Layer Stack Architecture
Layer 3: Output (Judgment)
What the market sees. Human judgment owns this entirely. Positioning docs, competitive battlecards, launch assets, category POVs, executive narratives.
Layer 2: Specialist (Intelligence)
Domain intelligence and synthesis. Agents produce raw material; PMM directs, filters, interprets. Win/loss analysis, competitor monitoring, voice-of-customer synthesis, market trend analysis.
Layer 1: Core AI (Velocity)
Automation and velocity. Fully AI-executed; PMM sets standards. Format conversion, research aggregation, first-draft generation, prompt library maintenance.
The judgment loop is the compounding mechanism. When a PMM reviews output and decides it's not sharp enough, that judgment feeds back into the prompts and frameworks governing the Specialist Layer.

Over time, the stack gets better because the PMM keeps improving the judgment loop.

The Three Moves

The transition to Full Stack is three sequential moves. The order matters: building the stack creates time for rebuilding the story, and the story work clarifies which skills are worth investing in.

1

Build Your Stack

Identify your highest-volume commodity tasks and build agent workflows for them. Not one grand AI strategy—five specific workflows that run without you. The stack is not a tool list; it's a set of running processes.

2

Rebuild Your Story

Revisit every major positioning claim and ask whether it is true, specific, and differentiated enough. This is the work that becomes possible when the stack is running. Sharp positioning, cleaner category narratives, more specific competitive differentiation.

3

Rebuild Your Skills

The craft skills that matter most are not the ones AI can replicate—they're the ones AI makes more valuable. Deep competitive analysis, category narrative construction, executive-level positioning judgment. These are the multipliers.

Why the Full Stack PMM Compounds

A PMM who builds a good stack and invests the recovered time in genuine craft development doesn't just get incrementally better—they get better in a way that improves the stack, which frees more time, which enables better craft development, which improves the stack further.

"The Full Stack PMM is not a destination you arrive at. It is a set of decisions you make—about what to automate, what to protect, what to get better at—and the compounding starts the moment you make the first one."

The PMMs who start this sequence now will not be caught up to later—because by the time others start, the compound effect will have moved the target.

Key Takeaways

  • The Full Stack PMM is not a generalist with tools—it's a deep specialist with AI-extended horizontal reach. The depth is irreplaceable; the horizontal is AI-enabled.
  • The AI-Enabled Workflow reallocates the same eight hours: commodity execution drops from ~50% to ~10%. Narrative and strategic work doubles.
  • The Depth/Breadth Matrix has four profiles. Most PMMs are Specialists or Generalists. The destination is Full Stack. The risk is ending up Obsolete.
  • The Stack Architecture has three layers—Core AI (velocity), Specialist (intelligence), Output (judgment). The judgment loop ties them together and improves them over time.
  • The three moves: Build Your Stack. Rebuild Your Story. Rebuild Your Skills. In that order, for that reason.
